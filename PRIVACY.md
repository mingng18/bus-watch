# BusWatch KL Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 12 July 2026

BusWatch KL is a standalone Apple Watch app for viewing nearby bus information in Kuala Lumpur and the Klang Valley. This policy explains how the app handles information.

## Information BusWatch processes

### Location

With your permission, BusWatch uses your Apple Watch's precise location to find nearby buses and bus stops. The coordinates are sent to the BusWatch service only to answer the current nearby-transit request.

BusWatch does not intentionally retain rider location in its application databases, associate location with an account, build a location history, use location for advertising or analytics, or sell location information.

You can deny or revoke location access in Apple Watch Settings. Nearby results require location access.

### Information stored on your device

BusWatch may store limited app preferences and cached transit schedules on your device or in its private App Group container. These records are used for app functionality and are not used for advertising or tracking.

### Network and service data

BusWatch does not require an account and does not intentionally collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, advertising identifiers, contacts, health information, payment details, or user-generated content.

The app communicates with the BusWatch backend, public-transit data providers, and Apple MapKit services. Infrastructure providers may process limited technical request information, such as IP address and request timing, for delivery, security, reliability, and abuse prevention under their own terms and privacy policies.

## Analytics, advertising, and tracking

BusWatch contains no third-party advertising SDK, behavioral analytics SDK, or cross-app tracking. It does not display ads or sell personal information.

## Sharing

BusWatch does not sell personal information. Information is transmitted only to service providers needed to operate the app, including cloud infrastructure and map/transit-data services, for the purposes described above.

## Retention

Rider coordinates used for nearby lookups are processed in real time and are not intentionally stored in BusWatch application databases. On-device preferences and cached transit data remain until replaced, removed by the app, or deleted when you remove the app.

## Children

BusWatch is a general-audience transit utility and is not directed specifically to children. It does not knowingly request personal information from children.

## Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect the service. No internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

## Changes

This policy may be updated when app behavior or legal requirements change. The effective date above will be revised when material changes are published.
