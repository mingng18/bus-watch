BusWatch KL puts Klang Valley public transit on your wrist.

Open the app for a quick view of nearby buses, bus stops and rail stations—without reaching for your phone.

FEATURES
• See live bus positions on a nearby map
• Check nearby bus and rail stops with distance and arrival estimates
• View upcoming rail departures at supported stations
• Follow supported bus trips stop by stop, including live bus position when available
• Choose nearby rail stations manually when location access is unavailable
• Read clear live, scheduled and approximate arrival indicators

BUILT FOR APPLE WATCH
BusWatch KL is a standalone Apple Watch experience designed for fast, glanceable transit checks around Kuala Lumpur and the Klang Valley.

PRIVACY BY DESIGN
No account, advertising or cross-app tracking. Your location is used only to answer real-time nearby-transit requests and is not retained in BusWatch application databases.

An internet connection is required for current transit information. Real-time data and arrival accuracy depend on availability from participating public-transport data sources.
